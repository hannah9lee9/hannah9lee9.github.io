The Four Roots at Magnolia Coffee

Welcome

It’s in that spirit of inclusion and creative possibilities that we welcome you. So whether you’re a regular or first timer, we hope you’ll crave our menu, be stimulated by the art and moved by the live music.

Serve

At Magnolia Coffee, we recognize that although coffee and art is part of the business, it's a whole lot more part of the service we're all about. Our passion is serving others nourishment of both the body and soul.

Inspire

We hope you’ll discover a space that nurtures a sense of community and creates the freedom to be and express yourself. We see Magnolia Coffee as a nod to the countless years of intrepid artists that ate, drank and imagined together.
